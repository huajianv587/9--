## 填写步骤

### **步骤1：打开文件**

### **步骤2：找到并替换所有 [TEMPLATE] 标记**

使用 Word 的"查找和替换"功能（Cmd+F / Ctrl+F），搜索：`[TEMPLATE`

你应该看到以下需要填写的字段：

---

### **字段1：作者姓名**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_AUTHOR_NAME]`

**替换为**：你的完整姓名（英文）

**示例**：
- `Jian Guo`
- `Huijian Wen`
- 或你在学术出版物中使用的标准英文名

**格式要求**：
- First Name + Last Name
- 不需要职称（Dr., Prof.等）

---

### **字段2：作者单位**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_AFFILIATION]`

**替换为**：你的机构全称（英文）

**示例**：
```
School of Economics, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
```

或
```
Business School, National University of Singapore, Singapore
```

**格式要求**：
- 院系/学院名称
- 大学全称
- 国家/地区

---

### **字段3：联系邮箱**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_EMAIL]`

**替换为**：你的学术邮箱

**示例**：
- `huijian.wen@ntu.edu.sg`
- `jian.guo@nus.edu.sg`

**格式要求**：
- 优先使用机构邮箱
- 确保这个邮箱你能及时收到编辑部邮件

---

### **字段4：ORCID（如果有）**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_ORCID]` 或 `ORCID: [TEMPLATE]`

**替换为**：
- 如果你有 ORCID：`0000-0001-2345-6789`（你的实际 ORCID）
- 如果没有：删除整行或写 `Not applicable`

**ORCID 说明**：
- ORCID 是学术身份识别号
- 可以在 https://orcid.org/ 免费注册
- 不是必须的，但强烈建议有

---

### **字段5：资助声明（Funding Statement）**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_FUNDING]`

**替换为**（选择一个）：

**情况A：有资助**
```
This work was supported by [基金名称] under Grant [编号].
```

**示例**：
```
This work was supported by the National Natural Science Foundation of China under Grant 72103456.
```

**情况B：无资助**
```
This research received no specific grant from any funding agency in the public, commercial, or not-for-profit sectors.
```

---

### **字段6：利益冲突声明（Conflict of Interest）**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_COI]`

**替换为**（通常选第一个）：

**情况A：无利益冲突（最常见）**
```
The author declares no conflict of interest.
```

**情况B：有利益冲突**
```
The author declares the following financial interests/personal relationships which may be considered as potential competing interests: [具体说明]
```

---

### **字段7：伦理审批（Ethics Approval）**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_ETHICS]` 或类似字段

**替换为**：

**对于这篇论文（使用公开数据库）**：
```
This study uses publicly available archival data from S&P Capital IQ and does not involve human subjects research. No ethics approval was required.
```

或简单地：
```
Not applicable. This study uses archival financial data only.
```

---

### **字段8：数据可用性补充（如果文件中有）**

**查找**：`[TEMPLATE_DATA_AVAILABILITY_DETAILS]`

**替换为**：
```
Aggregate summary statistics and audit reports are available in the submission materials. Replication code (excluding proprietary data) is available upon request.
```

## 完整示例

假设你的信息是：
- 姓名：Jian Guo
- 单位：School of Economics, Nanyang Technological University
- 邮箱：jian.guo@ntu.edu.sg
- ORCID：0000-0002-1234-5678
- 资助：无
- 利益冲突：无

填写后的 Title Page 应该显示：

```
Analyst Coverage and Subsequent Accounting-Based Financial Stress: 
Evidence from Singapore and Australia

Jian Guo
School of Economics, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
Email: jian.guo@ntu.edu.sg
ORCID: 0000-0002-1234-5678

Funding Statement:
This research received no specific grant from any funding agency in the 
public, commercial, or not-for-profit sectors.

Conflict of Interest Statement:
The author declares no conflict of interest.

Ethics Approval:
This study uses publicly available archival data from S&P Capital IQ and 
does not involve human subjects research. No ethics approval was required.

Data Availability:
The data used in this study were obtained from S&P Capital IQ under 
institutional database access and are subject to database licensing and 
redistribution restrictions. The author cannot redistribute raw Capital IQ 
exports or firm-level Capital IQ-derived panels. Researchers with their own 
Capital IQ access may reconstruct the sample using the reported variable 
definitions, sample filters, and replication code. Non-proprietary aggregate 
tables and audit outputs are included in the submission materials.
```

## 常见问题

### Q1: 我的单位名称很长，怎么办？
A: 可以适当简化，但保留核心信息：
- 完整：School of Business, Faculty of Economics and Management, University Name
- 简化：School of Business, University Name

### Q2: 我有多个单位，怎么写？
A: 
```
Jian Guo^{1,2}

^1 School of Economics, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
^2 Institute of Financial Research, National University of Singapore
```

### Q3: 可以用 Gmail 等非机构邮箱吗？
A: 可以，但不推荐。机构邮箱更专业，编辑部更倾向接受机构邮箱。

### Q4: ORCID 必须有吗？
A: 不是必须，但越来越多期刊要求。建议花 2 分钟注册一个：https://orcid.org/
