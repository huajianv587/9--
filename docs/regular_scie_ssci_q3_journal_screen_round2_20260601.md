# Regular SCIE/SSCI Q3 Journal Screen, Round 2

Date: 2026-06-01

## Screening Rule

The screen uses the user's current constraint: regular SCIE/SSCI JCR-level journal, Q3 preferred, not CAS/Chinese Academy zoning, reputation not obviously below a normal SSCI journal, and realistic fit for an empirical finance/accounting/economics paper on analyst coverage and future financial stress.

## Result

Round 2 does not overturn the main decision. Asia-Pacific Journal of Financial Studies remains the best primary target. The additional journals checked either have much lower public acceptance rates, weaker scope fit, or weaker reputation fit for this paper.

## Interpretation

The hard constraint remains unresolved at the single-journal level. A credible regular SSCI/JCR Q3 finance or economics journal with 70-80% acceptance was not found. The safer interpretation is:

- APJFS is the strongest normal-reputation Q3 primary target for this manuscript.
- APJAE is the best regular transfer candidate.
- AEL remains a compressed fallback, but its official public acceptance rate is 35%, so it cannot satisfy the user's stated 70-80% single-journal condition.
- Journals with public acceptance around 9-30% are not compatible with the user's 70-80% single-journal target even if their topical fit is plausible.
